Build. Active. Music.

a musical toy

8 Blocks. 8 Notes. Infinite possibilies.

With BAM You Build Your Music.

Build

BAM comes in 8 blocks. Designed with a pressure sensor inside, each lights up and plays a different note when you step on it. So you can stack them to make chords and spread them like a piano.

Active

We created BAM with activity in mind. So get up and get moving because with BAM the faster you move, the faster you play.

Music

With two ways to learn and play you can learn a new song or record your own. There are a ton of songs to choose from and once you create your own you can learn to play it again and again.

Two ways to learn and play

Composition Mode

Bluetooth connectivity allows you to see the notes you play on the screen while you compose your own music. So you can store your original songs and play them later.

Learning Mode

In learning mode you can select a song to play and the blocks will light up in the order you should play them. You can learn to play a preloaded song, your song, or even your friend's song.
